Who I am

“Fiat lux for cinema!”

I'm a Computer Science B.A. at the University of California, Berkeley. I'm currently interested in a variety of topics, in particular those dealing with computer graphics, machine learning, and the intersection of the two.

That said, I'm also a filmmaker and photographer and my goal in that space is to tell compelling stories about people and the world. I enjoy trying to capture a moment, a feeling, in time and communicating that feeling to the rest of the world.

My goal is ultimately to find the intersection between storytelling and technology to help other story tellers perfect their craft.
